The Estate

Where Luxury
Meets Nature

Perched above the Pacific, Casa Bali is a private sanctuary nestled in the Costa Rican rainforest canopy — just minutes from the legendary surf breaks of Dominical. Watch humpback whales breach from your morning coffee perch. Fall asleep to howler monkeys and tree frogs. Wake to toucans, hummingbirds, and the glittering ocean below. This is Pura Vida, elevated.
